# Cars & Kids

Nonprofit website for Cars & Kids - bringing incredible cars and shared experiences to kids in hospitals, schools, and communities.

## Mission

We believe shared visceral experiences transfer energy and momentum. Cars & Kids brings amazing cars directly to kids who need a spark of excitement, confidence, and joy.

## Deployment

This site is deployed via GitHub Pages and accessible at [carsandkids.net](https://carsandkids.net).

## Local Development

Simply open `index.html` in your browser to view the site locally.
