What We Do

We create moments that spark something inside kids—through the power of incredible machines and genuine human connection.

Visits

We bring amazing cars directly to hospitals, schools, and youth programs. No barriers, no waiting—excitement comes to them.

Events

High-energy gatherings where kids experience sound, motion, and presence. Feel the rumble. Hear the engine. Live the moment.

Connections

Meaningful moments between car enthusiasts and kids who need a spark. Real people sharing real passion.

Why It Matters

There's a fundamental energy to the universe. Our job is to transfer it—through shared experiences that ignite something lasting.

Energy

There's fundamental energy in the universe. We transfer it—from roaring engines to wide-eyed kids.

Confidence

A visceral experience can unlock something inside a kid. A moment of awe becomes a foundation of belief.

Memory

These moments become fuel for the future. Years later, they'll remember how it felt—and that feeling carries forward.

Joy

Pure, simple excitement that radiates outward. When a kid lights up, everyone around them feels it too.

Get Involved

Support Cars & Kids
