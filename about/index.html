About

Cool cars for kids who could use some excitement.

What we do

We show up with a handful of incredible cars—classics, exotics, whatever we've got—and let kids climb in, hear the engines, and just have a moment.

We visit hospitals, schools, and community programs. Wherever there are kids who could use a lift, we try to be there with something worth seeing. No agenda. Just cars and good energy.

What we believe

We believe in a fundamental energy and momentum. Sometimes what kids need is a little fun and excitement for empowerment—a moment that breaks the routine and lights something up inside them.

We believe in the visceral experience of a car—auditory, visual, and physical—having the power to invigorate. The rumble of an engine, the gleam of the paint, the feeling of sitting behind the wheel. These things stick with you.
